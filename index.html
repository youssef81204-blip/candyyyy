About Candy

A Legacy of Luxury

Candy is a premium luxury food brand that has redefined the confectionery industry. With world-class quality, elegant packaging, and modern branding, we deliver an unparalleled experience to our customers.

Our mission is to create moments of pure joy through exceptional confectionery. Every product is crafted with the finest ingredients sourced from around the world.

Our vision is to become the world's most recognized luxury confectionery brand, setting new standards in taste, quality, and elegance.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We offer premium delivery across all major cities in Egypt. Orders above 200 EGP qualify for free delivery.

Are your products suitable for gifting?

Absolutely! All our products come in luxury packaging. We also offer special gift collections and custom gift wrapping services.

What are your return and refund policies?

We accept returns within 24 hours of delivery for unopened products. Please contact our customer service for assistance.

Do you offer corporate orders?

Yes! We provide custom corporate gifting solutions with branded packaging and bulk discounts. Contact us for a personalized quote.

Where do you source your ingredients?

We source our premium ingredients from the finest suppliers worldwide — Belgian chocolate, Italian hazelnuts, Colombian coffee beans, and more.
